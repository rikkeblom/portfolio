Hi!

My name is Rikke. I'm an aspiring multimedia designer and frontend developer.

I've always been fascinated by the world of digital communication and especially the way frontend development merges logic and creativity. I started my journey at the University of Southern Denmark where I got my BA in International Business Communication and became the communication manager of Erasmus Student Network Odense. During this time it became clear to me that it wouldn't be enough to just design and write for the web
- I wanted to create it.

Today I'm studying multimedia design with specialization in frontend development at Copenhagen school of Design and Technology (aug 2020- aug 2022). If you scroll down I'd love to show you some of my projects.
