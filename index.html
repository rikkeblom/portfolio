Nishiki Sushi

React site w. takeaway ordering system and table booking

I created this site in collaboration with 2 classmates as our final project for AP in Multimedia Design. The project is based around the sushi restaurant Nishiki Sushi located in Frederiksund and involved:

  • market and competitor research and analysis,
  • target group analysis,
  • audit of their current solution,
  • creating a new visual identity guide,
  • wireframing,
  • prototyping,
  • developing the new site in react:
    • contact form,
    • database of dishes,
    • takeaway system,
    • checkout forms,
    • table ordering system
  • and testing of both the figma prototype and developed site
